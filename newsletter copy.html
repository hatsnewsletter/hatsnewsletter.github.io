Revolutionizing Motion Retargeting for Legged Robots with Differentiable Optimal Control

The article by Disney Research discusses a new method for copying movements from animals or animations onto robots with legs, using a technique that makes these movements more accurate and efficient. This advancement is important for improving how robots move in fields like robotics, animation, and gaming.

Creative Technologies Talks - Aalto University

The Creative Technologies Talks hosted by Aalto University offer a series of videos featuring experts from universities and organizations such as KTH, Aalto University, and Imperial College London. These presentations delve into various aspects of creative technologies, including AI in music, performance, games, and art, as well as advancements in extended reality (XR) and computational design.
