CT scan of a SawStop tablesaw safety mechanism

We had posted a CT scan teardown to the newsletter before, but we like these so much that we’re posting another. This time they analyze a SawStop system, which is a device that stops tablesaws when it detects skin contacting the saw. This device has to function well to save human lives (and avoid false triggers, since that ruins your saw for no reason), learn how it works in this post.

“Natural Balance” Flower Pot Design

This flowerpot designed by Studio Lorier, contains a water reservoir that gradually feeds the plant over weeks. When the reservoir is empty, the pot tilts to indicate a refill is required.

FreeCAD Version 1.0 Released

After more than 20 years of development, the FreeCAD community releases version 1.0. This major milestone introduces a stable and feature-rich platform. Key features include tools for Building Information Modeling (BIM) to design buildings, CAM for machining simulations, and FEM for testing structural strength all directly within the FreeCAD.

What Happens When You Break the Rules of Tape Loops? - iturnknobs

Cassettes have two sides and ends, and once one side is finished, you flip it around. Looper tapes offer a continuous playback with a single loop of magnetic tape, but their length is typically short. Increasing the length is a topological challenge against tape bundling. In this video, iturnknobs explores a concept inspired by the Roland RE-201 to extend loop length by several minutes. But can you double the length of playback using the second side of the tape, much like a Möbius strip?

Oᵒ Shoe made with biodegradable organic polymers by OXMAN

OXMAN introduced a new shoe made by PHA) thermoplastics that are synthetized by bacteria that and are one of the most biodegradable polymers. They customize and design the shoe parametrically into a a variety of thicknesses, densities shapes and mechanical behaviors with their in-house software. Later the shoe is woven-printed onto a form using a 5 axis 3d printer on a form with a textile base using the PHA filaments.
