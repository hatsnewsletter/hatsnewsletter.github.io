Is cat purring chaotic? -Not David

Not David investigates the science of cat purring, examining whether it is a chaotic or orderly process involving muscle, nerve, and brain coordination. It highlights the diversity in purring patterns among cats, linking this to chaos theory and its prevalence in many other phenomena.

Fun command line tools by Charm

Charm creates intuitive and elegant command line tools for activities such as sending emails, reading content, storing data, and terminal applications. They offer all these tools openly for public use.

Teardown Library: a community of product designers searching for inspiration

Teardown Library is a community-driven platform offering resources for product designers, including workshops, meetups, online forums, and design challenges. It provides hands-on experience by exploring various products, fostering skill improvement and networking opportunities for designers.

picoSYNTH by Johan Peitz

Created by Johan Peitz, PicoSYNTH makes creating simple loops and beats fun. The click and drag interface enables you to combine the equipment in interesting ways making your own analog synths!

USB-C cables head-to-head teardown by Lumafield

Lumafiels has CT scanned USB-C cables currently on the market. You can view the 3D CT scan data. They present their analysis and compare them against eachother.
