Beyond Manual Dexterity: Designing a Multi-fingered Robotic Hand for Grasping and Crawling by Xiao GAO

Researchers from EPFL demonstrate a robotic arm with a custom hand for grasping and accessing objects on a surface. They designed a hand that can grasp on both sides and train it with genetic algorithms in MuJoCo Sim. The hand can detach automatically, crawl to reach objects, pick it up, crawl back and re-attach.
