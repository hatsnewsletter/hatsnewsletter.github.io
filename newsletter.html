12,419 Days Of Strandbeest Evolution - Veritasium

Theo Jansen conceived the idea of Strandbeests to help build dunes against rising sea levels by kicking up sand. Using genetic algorithms, he optimized their leg geometry and walking paths. For the past 30 years, he has been building, evolving and testing these mechanical creatures as if they were living animals, strolling the beach.
